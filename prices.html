Funding

Bella Santosha's Studio recgonizes movement should be accessible to everyone. Payment plans are available as well as scholarships and a product barter system. Karmi team member positions are available, a karmi is someone who helps clean and prepare the studio for free classes. Not only do you get free unlimited classes but you also get to be part of an awesome community. We have parterned up with Mirabella Feed & Seed as well as BearSpring Farm for scholarships and farm karmi positions as they are a community orientated buisness investing value in the people of Vilna as well as its commuity.
